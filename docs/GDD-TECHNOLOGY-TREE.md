## RESEARCH OVERVIEW

Technology advances through **Research Points (RP)** allocated to tech tree nodes. Each nation starts with different baseline technology levels reflecting real-world capabilities. Research is organized into **6 branches**, each with 4 tiers.

**Research Points Generation:**
```
RP/turn = (R&D Budget % × GDP) + Allied Tech Sharing Bonus
        + University Level + Espionage Bonus (stolen tech)
        - (War Damage to research facilities)
```

**Research Speed Modifiers:**
- Peacetime bonus: +20% RP efficiency (less disruption)
- War economy: -15% RP (focus on production)
- Allied research pact: +10–25% RP for shared nodes
- Brain drain (economic crisis): -20% RP
- Tech transfer (purchased or stolen): -50% cost on specific node

## BRANCH 1: CONVENTIONAL LAND WARFARE

### TIER 1 — BASELINE (all nations start with these)
- **Modern Doctrine** — Combined arms coordination basic bonus (+10%)
- **GPS Navigation** — All units +5% accuracy, +10% march speed
- **Night Vision Equipment** — Remove night combat penalty for equipped units
- **IED/Mine Countermeasures** — Reduce logistics route disruption

### TIER 2 — ADVANCED CONVENTIONAL (200 RP)
- **Precision Artillery** — MLRS/SPG accuracy +25%; enable GPS-guided shells
- **Active Protection Systems** — MBTs gain 40% missile interception
- **Tactical Drone Integration** — Brigade-level ISR drones; reveal 2-hex radius
- **Urban Combat Doctrine** — Infantry +20% in urban terrain; civilian casualty reduction
- **Digital Battlefield Network** — All units share intelligence; +15% combat coordination
- **Hypersonic Maneuver** — Infantry vehicles +2 hex/day; logistics +20%

### TIER 3 — NEAR-FUTURE (500 RP)
- **AI-Assisted Targeting** — +30% artillery accuracy; first shot kill probability +20%
- **Autonomous Ground Vehicles** — Deploy unmanned combat vehicles (half cost, no manpower)
- **Directed Energy (Land)** — DEW systems; unlimited ammo vs drone swarms
- **Exoskeleton Infantry** — Infantry +20% combat; can carry heavier loads; -20% fatigue
- **Quantum Communication** — Immune to enemy EW jamming
- **Hypersonic Interceptor (Land)** — Boost Tier 3 SAM capability vs hypersonic threats

### TIER 4 — SPECULATIVE/2030+ (1200 RP)
- **Fully Autonomous Combat Units** — AI-controlled units; no morale modifier; controversy mechanics
- **Railgun Artillery** — Extreme range (500km), negligible ammo cost, high power requirement
- **Energy Shield Prototypes** — Experimental; +60% defense vs unguided weapons
- **Swarm Intelligence** — Coordinate 1,000+ mini-drones as a single tactical unit

## TECHNOLOGY SHARING & THEFT

**Alliance Tech Sharing:**
- Allied nations can share research nodes (both benefit)
- Sharing reduces individual RP cost by 30–50%
- Requires active military alliance

**Technology Espionage:**
- Successful intel operation can steal a completed tech node
- Recipient gets node at 50% cost (still needs time to integrate)
- Detected theft: -15 global reputation; possible sanctions

**Technology Export Controls:**
- Nations can embargo specific technologies
- Breaking embargo: reputation cost + possible sanctions from third parties
- Dual-use technology (space launch = missile; civilian nuclear = weapons-grade material)
