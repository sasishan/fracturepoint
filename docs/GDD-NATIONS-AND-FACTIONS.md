### DYNAMIC ALLIANCE FORMATION
- Players can propose, join, or fracture alliances mid-game
- Alliance stability is tracked (0–100); stressed by conflicting interests
- **Betrayal mechanics**: Breaking an alliance triggers -50 global reputation and potential defection of minor allies
- **Neutrality**: Nations can declare neutrality (Switzerland model); attacking neutrals causes major diplomatic penalties

### ALIGNMENT SPECTRUM
Each nation has an alignment score (-100 to +100) for each major bloc:
- +100 = Full vassal/ally
- 0 = Truly neutral
- -100 = Active enemy

Actions that shift alignment:
- Military aid / arms sales
- Economic investment / sanctions
- Propaganda campaigns
- Assassination of hostile leaders
- Liberation vs. occupation of territory
- Humanitarian aid

## BLOC IDEOLOGY SYSTEM

| Ideology        | Bonus                              | Penalty                        |
|----------------|------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Liberal Democracy** | Highest economic output; best recruitment of allies | War fatigue fastest; public approval constrains military options |
| **Autocratic Nationalism** | No war fatigue; suppress dissent; full military mobilization | Sanctions hurt more; limited alliance options |
| **State Capitalism** | Fastest industrialization; tech catch-up bonus | Debt accumulation; corruption reduces efficiency |
| **Theocracy** | Ideological fighters (+15% morale); proxy network | International isolation; economic inefficiency |

## NATIONAL MORALE SYSTEM

Each nation has a **National Morale** score (0–100) affecting:
- Unit combat effectiveness (+/- 25% at extremes)
- Civilian production output (+/- 30%)
- Political stability (below 30 = coup/revolution risk)
- Willingness to continue war (below 20 = armistice pressure)

**Morale modifiers:**
- Military victories: +5–15
- Military defeats: -5–20
- Nuclear attack (any): -30 (all nations)
- Enemy propaganda: -2–10
- War crimes / civilian atrocities: -15 (own nation morale)
- Economic hardship: -1 per turn below pre-war GDP
- Liberation of homeland territory: +10–25
